Privacy policy

Shasta Regional Community Foundation is committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity and security of personal information entrusted to us by those who visit our website.

Like all organizations with an online presence, Shasta Regional Community Foundation receives standard tracking data: information from your browser, including your IP address and the page you visited, is recorded in our website server logs. This data is analyzed to determine how to best provide information to visitors to our website. No personal information is collected during the tracking process.

Information that you provide us by filling out online forms is located on a secure server. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Once you provide us with information, that data remains confidential. We will not share any information you provide with any individual or organization.

We do not collect or maintain information from those we know are younger than age 13, and no part of our website is designed to attract anyone under 13.

Online giving

Shasta Regional Community Foundation works with Network for Good to process online donations. Network for Good uses industry-leading Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to keep your personal information as secure as possible. Network for Good helps to protect your information by working with partners that provide a secure and safe environment for credit card donations.

At a minimum, the information you provide when giving online is used to complete your donation. With your permission, Network for Good can also provide your name and contact information to Shasta Regional Community Foundation. If you do not wish to share your name and contact information with us or you choose to make your donation anonymously, Network for Good will not share your name or contact information. In either case, you will receive a thank you confirmation from Network for Good that can be used as a record of your contribution.

If you choose to allow Network for Good to share your information with us, that data remains confidential. We will not share any information you provide with any individual or organization.

More information about the privacy and security of giving to the community foundation through Network for Good can be found on its website (http://support.networkforgood.org/Default.asp?c=11).
