Stewardship Endowment Fund

We offer donors a wide variety of options for giving?establishing permanent endowments, creating flexible donor-advised funds, joining with others in addressing important issues through our ?field-of-interest? areas or supporting our newly-established Stewardship Fund.

The Stewardship Fund at the Shasta Regional Community Foundation is our most versatile and responsive fund. The Stewardship Fund was established in order to support:

  • Programs that address our most pressing local needs.
  • Emerging opportunities that improve our local quality of life.
  • The Community Foundation?s on-going efforts to increase philanthropic endeavors throughout Shasta and Siskiyou Counties.
