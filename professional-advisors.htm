Professional Advisors

Partners in philanthropy

Shasta Regional Community Foundation can help you and your clients meet charitable goals across the entire region. If you have clients who can benefit from making charitable gifts, but you want to avoid recommending specific causes or organizations, partnering with the Community Foundation can give your clients a wide range of opportunities to address the issues they care about most, while receiving the maximum tax benefit under law.

Why work through the Community Foundation

  • We work through you. You continue to build your client relationships, and we help you serve their charitable giving needs.
  • We partner with you. We provide support, information and expertise around charitable giving options. We offer as much, or as little, support as you need, every step of the way.
  • We help you build stronger relationships. You help your clients achieve charitable impact and receive tax advantages by working with us.
  • We help you connect across generations. When you help families establish advised endowment funds at the Community Foundation, you begin a legacy of involvement with current and future generations.

Investing for the benefit of our communities

Shasta Regional Community Foundation's Investment Policy Statement ensures that appropriate investment strategies are used to manage risk and ensure that charitable dollars grow and are available forever to benefit our communities. The Investment Committee includes Board Members and professionals in our community who have significant experience and knowledge in finance and investing.
