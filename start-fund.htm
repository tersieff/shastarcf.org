Start a Fund

Shasta Regional Community Foundation offers great giving options. We can help you choose the one that?s right for you.

You can give any amount to an existing fund. With a gift of $10,000 or more, you can establish a new fund. Funds can be established in your name, in the name of a loved one, or the name of a special cause or organization.

Types of charitable funds

  • Unrestricted funds meet ever-changing community needs
  • Field of interest funds connect personal values to high-impact opportunities
  • Designated funds help local organizations sustain and grow
  • Organizational endowments help nonprofit organizations invest in their future and long-term financial health
  • Donor advised funds offer a personalized approach to giving
  • Scholarships invest in students
  • Tribute gifts honor community members and commemorate important events
